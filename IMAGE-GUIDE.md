## üõ† Free Image Optimization Tools

### Compress Images (Reduce File Size):
1. **TinyPNG** - https://tinypng.com
   - Drag and drop
   - Reduces file size by 50-80% without visible quality loss
   - Use this for ALL photos before uploading

2. **Squoosh** - https://squoosh.app
   - More control over compression
   - Compare before/after

### Resize Images:
1. **Bulk Resize Photos** - https://bulkresizephotos.com
   - Resize multiple images at once
   - Set exact dimensions (e.g., 800x600)

2. **ILoveIMG** - https://www.iloveimg.com/resize-image
   - Batch resize
   - Crop to aspect ratio

### Convert Videos:
1. **CloudConvert** - https://cloudconvert.com
   - Convert MP4 to WebM
   - Compress videos
   - Free for up to 25 conversions/day

### Edit Photos:
1. **Photopea** - https://www.photopea.com
   - Free online Photoshop alternative
   - Add text, adjust colors, crop

2. **Canva** - https://www.canva.com
   - Great for social media images
   - Templates for og-image

## üì± Getting Great Photos

### DIY Photography Tips:

**Lighting:**
- Shoot during "golden hour" (1 hour after sunrise or before sunset)
- Avoid harsh midday sun
- For interiors: turn on sauna lights, use natural window light

**Composition:**
- Include context (trees, deck, landscape)
- Show scale (include people if possible)
- Capture details (wood grain, steam, water droplets)
- Get wide shots AND close-ups

**What to Photograph:**

1. **Exterior Shots:**
   - Full sauna in backyard setting
   - Multiple angles
   - Seasonal variety (summer, winter with snow)
   - With and without people

2. **Interior Shots:**
   - Benches and seating
   - Heater and rocks
   - Wood grain details
   - Steam/ambiance (during use)

3. **Installation Process:**
   - You and team working
   - Site prep
   - Construction details
   - Before/after transformations

4. **Customer Projects:**
   - Completed installations
   - Happy customers (with permission)
   - Different settings and styles

**Equipment:**
- Modern smartphone is fine!
- Clean the lens first
- Use HDR mode for high-contrast scenes
- Take LOTS of photos, pick the best later

### Professional Photography Option:
Consider hiring a local Peterborough photographer for 2-3 hours:
- Cost: $300-500
- Get 30-50 professional photos
- Great investment for your website and marketing
- Can use for years

1. Open your GitHub repository

3. Click "Add file" ‚Üí "Upload files"

2. Go to Netlify dashboard
3. Drag updated folder to deploy area
